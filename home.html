Our Work

EXAMPLES OF OUR GRAPHIC DESIGN WORK

  • Our logo for a chocolatier based out of New Delhi. The logo integrates their product along with the name of the company in an extremely functional yet minimalistic design.
  • This logo was created for a pro-bono legal entrepreneurship cell. The initials of the cell (LEC) are incorporated into the logo itself, while focusing on their core services.
  • A logo for an Annual University Sports Fest that was specifically created keeping in mind the gladiator theme of the event.
  • The logo for the Indian Entertainment and Law Blog - the design merges the law factor (the scales of justice) and the film aspect (the film strip and the camera), along with the Indian flag at the bottom.
  • Another law focused logo - the Centre for Penology, Criminal Justice, and Police Studies.
  • This is a logo for a t-shirt design for a University Cultural Fest that inculcates the rural and local theme.
